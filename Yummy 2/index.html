HELP THE NEEDY

Save Food Share Food an organization based in Nashik. We ensure the food in events is not wasted and provided to the NGOs.

  • Minimize food wastages
  • Create a community for effective utilization of waste food as a resource.

    • Mission: We ensure every grain of cooked food reaches an empty stomach.

Surplus Food Sharing

Coalition of Partners to prevent food waste and food loss. We create an adequate facility and supply chain to provide food to the NGOs based in Nashik.

Learn More

With 10.4% of world's total food production India is the second largest food producer after China.

Inspite of this India has 196 million under-nourished people second highest in the world.

An efficient supply chain is the only solution to this wastage and hunger.
